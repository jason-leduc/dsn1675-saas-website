Is this a long-term commitment?

No. It's a pay-as-you-go service. You can cancel any time via one click on your account page. If you cancel, you'll be able to use paid features till the end of the paid month (30 days since your last payment).

Currently we accept Visa, Mastercard, and American Express. We do not accept PayPal. At this time we only accept payments online so we will not be able to accept a P.O., invoice from you, or take an order over the phone.

Are there any hidden charges?

There are no hidden charges. Monthly price gives you access to paid features of all the tabs on Tangle String. We are confident that you'll enjoy your subscription and we'll give you a 100% "no questions asked" refund otherwise.
